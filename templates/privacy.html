Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service: your name, email address, and the expense records you create. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any information beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and display your personal expense tracker. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose other than rendering the service back to you. We will never sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not integrate third-party advertising or analytics services that would share your data externally. Any external services used (such as fonts or hosting infrastructure) are limited to those necessary for the app to function and are governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond to privacy-related requests within a reasonable timeframe.
